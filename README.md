### About Me

I’m a **senior full-stack developer**, with more than **8 years of experience** across back-end, front-end, and mobile.

Over the years, I’ve worked at companies such as **Banco Safra, Linx, Consigaz, Ultragaz, Onebrain, Sympla, Vylex**, and **Medcof**.

I’ve also worked as a **tech lead** on development teams, leading projects that resulted in the release of **2 mobile apps** published on both the **App Store** and **Google Play**, reaching more than **55,000 downloads** in total.

I enjoy building scalable solutions, collaborating with high-performance teams, and delivering products with quality, performance, and a strong user experience.
